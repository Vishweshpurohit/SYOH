Sell Your Own Home (SYOH)

Skip the seller’s agent.
Keep the 4–6% commission.

SYOH is a guided, flat-fee platform that helps U.S. homeowners sell their own homes—without a traditional listing agent. We turn the complex steps of pricing, listing, showings, negotiation, and paperwork into a clear, step-by-step workflow.

On a $500,000 home, sellers can save up to $25,000+ in commission.

The problem with traditional home selling

Residential real estate is still sold using a high-percentage commission model, even though much of the process is now digital and standardized.

High commission, low transparency

  • Typical total commission: 4–6% of the home sale price.
  • Seller’s agent and buyer’s agent split the commission.
  • Fee is tied to price, not to the actual effort involved.

Misaligned incentives

  • Agents are often incentivized to close quickly, not to maximize net proceeds.
  • Long, restrictive listing agreements limit seller control.
  • Many tasks are repeatable and could be guided by software.
Our insight: most of the seller agent’s work—pricing from comparables, creating listings, scheduling showings, sharing documents, and navigating offers—can be broken into simple, guided steps that a motivated homeowner can follow with the right tools.

How SYOH works

SYOH replaces the traditional seller’s agent with a structured, digital workflow plus access to professionals only when you need them.

1. Price your home

Use data-driven comparables and local market trends to estimate a realistic listing price. SYOH guides you through setting a strategy, not just a number.

2. Create and publish your listing

A step-by-step listing wizard helps you write the description, upload photos, and highlight key features. Your listing can be prepared for major online portals and buyer channels.

3. Manage showings

Share available time slots, confirm appointments, and manage in-person or virtual tours from one dashboard, without endless back-and-forth.

4. Review offers & negotiate

SYOH helps you compare offers, understand contingencies, and structure counteroffers. You stay in control while following a clear decision framework.

5. Close with confidence

When legal documents and closing steps require professional help, SYOH connects you to attorneys, title companies, or transaction coordinators—only when needed, not by default.

Frequently asked questions

SYOH doesn’t replace every professional in the process—only the pieces that can be standardized and guided by software.

Do I still need a buyer’s agent?
Buyers may still choose to work with an agent. SYOH focuses on the seller side of the transaction, helping you avoid a full listing commission while staying prepared to handle incoming offers from represented or unrepresented buyers.
Is SYOH a replacement for legal advice?
No. SYOH is designed as a workflow and guidance platform. For contracts, state-specific forms, and legal questions, we recommend working with qualified real-estate attorneys. SYOH aims to make that collaboration easier and more focused.
What if I don’t have time to manage everything myself?
You remain in control, but SYOH can connect you with à la carte services like photography, staging, or transaction coordination. Instead of paying a percentage commission, you pay only for the specific help you need.
What stage is the SYOH project in?
SYOH is currently being developed as a platform concept and prototype, focusing on breaking down the seller’s journey into clear, tech-enabled steps. This site is a concept landing page that explains the vision.
